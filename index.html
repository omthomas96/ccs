A CONTAINER FOR EVERY OCCASION

Welcome to Creative Container Solutions, where sustainability meets functionality. We design and build high-quality, eco-friendly shipping container spaces tailored for living, working, and adventuring. Our spaces are built to last, thoughtfully designed, and fully customizable to suit your lifestyle. Whether you're setting up a mobile office, embracing tiny home living, or seeking an off-grid escape, CCS delivers smart, modern container solutions that do more with less.

Built From the Ground (and Steel) Up

Creative Container Solutions was founded by Jay Thomas, a builder with over 40 years of industry experience. What began as a single self-built container project quickly evolved into a full-fledged mission: to provide durable, affordable, and sustainable spaces for everyday life. We still operate out of that very first container Jay built—proof that when you start with strength and intention, you build something that lasts. At CCS, we believe in combining expert craftsmanship with environmentally conscious practices. Each container is transformed with care, using repurposed materials, efficient systems, and clever design principles that maximize every square foot. Every project we take on is built around real-life needs. From compact, portable offices to full-time tiny homes and weekend eco-retreats, our containers are as adaptable as they are reliable. Each unit is thoughtfully designed for comfort, utility, and sustainability, with options for off-grid living, solar integration, rainwater systems, and more. If you can imagine it, we can build it.

Why Choose CCS

At CCS, we don’t cut corners. We build with purpose and pride. Our containers are not only tough and travel-ready—they’re tailored to you. With decades of building experience behind us, a deep respect for the environment, and a passion for functional design, we’re proud to offer container solutions that are both practical and personal. From your first idea to your finished container, we’re here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you’re starting a new lifestyle or building your next workspace, Creative Container Solutions is ready to help you make it real.
