A CONTAINER FOR EVERY OCCASION

Here at Creative Container Solutions (CCS), we focus on creating sustainable, practical and comfortable living and working environments with an affordable price tag without cutting corners on quality.

CEO and Founder, Jay Thomas, has over 40 years of experience in the building industry and began this enterprise by building a Container of his own. Today, CCS still run out of that very first container, and our methods and materials have only improved.

Whether you are looking for a sturdy mobile office, a sustainable off-the-grid camping experience, or you are living the tiny home life, Creative Container Solutions has a solution you you.
